52°North WPS Quickstart¶

Running¶

  • To run 52°North WPS on the Live DVD, click on the “start 52NorthWPS” link on the desktop or you will find it in the menu-items.
  • Firefox will open the 52WPS-TestClient.
  • Push the Send-Button and the request will be transmitted to the 52°North WPS which will generate a xml-representation for a buffer around playgrounds with a width of 20.
  • In Order to build your own requests you will need the capabilities of the 52°North WPS:

    http://localhost:8083/wps/WebProcessingService?Request=GetCapabilities&Service=WPS

  • For further questions you can contact the 52°North WPS mailing list: geoprocessingservices@52north.org
